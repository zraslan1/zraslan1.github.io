I designed this ArcGIS Online Dashboard to focus on current Electric Vehicle Charging Stations in the USA and Canada. This data is maintained by an Aggregated Live Feed routine that accesses the US Department of Energy's National Renewable Energy Laboratory (NREL) API website on a daily basis. For an overview of the data you see in this map, visit the Alternative Fuel Station feature layer.
